FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How quickly can you come out?
We offer same-day and next-day service in most cases. For same-day, call us before noon. We'll do our best to work around your schedule and give you a 2-hour arrival window.
How is pricing determined?
We price by volume — the space your junk takes up in our truck. Our crew will assess on-site and give you an exact price before starting. No hourly rates, no hidden fees.
Do I need to be home during the removal?
Not always. As long as we can access the items and you've approved the quote in advance, we can complete the job and send you a photo confirmation when done.
What items can't you take?
We can't take hazardous waste, chemicals, paint, asbestos, propane tanks, or medical waste. If you're unsure about an item, just ask and we'll let you know. Most things we can handle.
Do you recycle or donate items?
Yes! We partner with local donation centers and recycling facilities to keep as much out of the landfill as possible. We'll always try to find a second home for items in good condition.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Moore 4 Less Junk Removal is fully licensed and insured for your protection. We carry liability insurance on every job so you have complete peace of mind.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes, always. You can submit a request online or call us. Our on-site quote is completely free with no obligation. You decide whether to proceed after seeing the price.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit cards, Venmo, Cash App, and Zelle. Payment is collected after the job is completed to your satisfaction.
